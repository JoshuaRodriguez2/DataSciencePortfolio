My Dream Position

My Dream Position

When I fantasize of my dream position in the Data Science World, it includes responsibilities that leak into all sectors of the business process. The profession of Data Science is much like an artist to me. An artist in a sense that they must be given data, dissect it, extract meaning, and then be able to convey the meaning to others in layman terms. There’s a beauty in overseeing every step in your process. My dream position would not only include the technical role of collecting, manipulating, and extracting insight from data. My Ability and Interest for Learning

My Ability and Interest for Learning

With the field of Data Science toolbox always growing, one of the most valuable skillsets to develop is the ability to learn quickly and adapt to new tools. When I was hired at my most recent job, I was told that I would be working with primarily Analytics and Data Pipeline, however with budget being tight and the company needing work on their in-development software, I took a step forward and said that I could aid in the front-end development despite never having experience in HTML, SCSS, or typescript (What the software was composed of). While initially quite stressful, within a month I developed the proficiency to by myself develop two company websites, and a software for our Carriers (Truck Drivers). While I may not currently be proficient in every Data Science tool that exists, I don't believe that there is a tool that exists that I couldn't take on.
